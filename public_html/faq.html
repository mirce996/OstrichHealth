What’s with the ostrich?

The ostrich is a proud, elegant bird, and our name is inspired
by the idea that we all like the confidence boost that comes
with feeling beautiful. Additionally, ostrich antibodies are
now used to enhance human skincare products, which we
find to be an incredible scientific breakthrough and another
source of inspiration. (No ostriches were harmed in the
formulation of our products).

How does nutrition impact my hair’s health?

Like the rest of your body, the health of your hair is
dependent on consistent intake of the right nutrients. When
vitamins or other crucial nutrients are lacking or hair
becomes damaged through styling, healthy growth is
diminished. But the good news is that you can nourish your
hair back to health.

How do I use your hair growth supplement?

For best results, take two capsules daily as a dietary
supplement after mealtime. Continue daily for 3-6 months for
optimal results.

How does the hair growth supplement work?

A proprietary blend of vitamins, minerals, amino acids and
other vital nutrients combine in this easy-to-take oral
supplement to form a nutritional powerhouse for growing
healthy, vibrant hair. Used over time, it restores your hair to
healthy, shiny thickness.
